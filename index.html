How To Play

You take command of a small white triangle ship that shoots projectiles. Take out as many enemy shapes as you can and get the high score.

  • Wander Enemies wander aimlessly
  • Follower Enemies will slowly follow you
  • Avoider Enemies will follow you and try to avoid your incoming bullets

Pressing the SPACEBAR will use one of your bombs and wipe the board of enemies.

Controls

  • W: move up
  • A: move left
  • S: move down
  • D: move right
  • ← rotate left
  • → rotate right
  • R: restart game
  • (spacebar): bomb
  • P: pause game
